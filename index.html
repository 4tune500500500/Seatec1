recision at Depth. Innovation at Sea. Seatec Services Inc. brings over two decades of hands-on subsea expertise to the world’s most challenging marine environments. Projects

A selection of our subsea engineering and ROV/AUV projects around the globe.

Subsea Telecoms
IT Telecom – RovJet 605
Canadian subsea telecom cable installation and maintenance using the RovJet 605 system. Scope included deep-water route survey, cable lay, post-lay burial, and emergency repair readiness. Seatec supplied offshore project engineering, pilot/technician teams, and 24/7 operations support from 2024 onwards.
Pipeline Trenching
Pharos Offshore – SMD Q800 Trencher
Deployment of the SMD Q800 jet-trencher off Sicily for the burial of subsea pipelines. Our team handled mobilization, ROV integration, real-time trenching control, and post-installation survey ensuring specification compliance and minimal seabed impact.
Pre-lay Route Survey
Argeo – Kongsberg Hugin Astral AUV
High-resolution pre-lay route surveys for offshore pipeline corridor in Brazil using autonomous underwater vehicles (AUVs). Delivered bathymetric, geophysical and environmental data to optimize cable and pipeline design, reducing installation risk and cost.
Research & Salvage
Pelagic Research Services – Odyssius ROV
Provided deep-water ROV operations for archeological and salvage work in the Pacific and Atlantic oceans. Tasks included artifact recovery, wreck site mapping, biological sampling, and live video transmission for onshore research teams.
Shipwreck Recovery
Deep Ocean Search – SS City of Cairo
Assisted in the deepwater recovery of precious metals from the WWII shipwreck “SS City of Cairo” at ~5,000 m depth. Managed heavy-lift ROV tooling, survey sensors, and safe recovery protocols under extreme conditions.
ROV & Scientific Support
Nautilus Live – Benthic Crawler “Wally”
Support for benthic crawler “Wally” and Nautilus ROV operations at deep-sea observatories. Integrated environmental sensors, managed fiber-optic umbilicals, and provided 24/7 subsea engineering during live broadcasts to the public.
Extreme Environments
Hydrothermal Vents Exploration
Deep-sea exploration of hydrothermal vent systems using advanced ROVs and sampling equipment. Collected geochemical and biological samples at depths over 3,000 m, contributing to research on chemosynthetic ecosystems.
Offshore Oil & Gas
Oceaneering – Petrobras Subsea Robotics Contract
Multi-year support contract supplying ROV pilots and subsea engineers for Petrobras operations. Provided intervention tooling, subsea metrology, and emergency response teams across multiple deepwater assets offshore Brazil.
Robotics & Autonomous Systems
Nauticus Robotics & Advanced Ocean Systems – MSA
Collaboration on multi-sensor autonomous platforms integrating ROV and AUV capabilities. Our role included sensor fusion, subsea control software, and offshore testing to validate hybrid operations.
Hybrid Platforms
Open Ocean & Nauticus Collaboration
Supporting development of hybrid ROV/AUV platforms for subsea inspection and monitoring. Focused on low-carbon operations, extended endurance, and advanced AI for anomaly detection.
Marine Archaeology
Ancient Shipwreck – Adrasan, Turkey
Delicate ROV inspections and 3D photogrammetry of an ancient shipwreck off Adrasan, Turkey. Delivered HD imagery for artifact cataloguing and site preservation planning with local authorities and researchers.
AUV/ROV Survey
Pipeline Route Survey – North Sea
Combined AUV and ROV operations for route survey and environmental baseline assessment in the North Sea. Delivered integrated datasets for pipeline engineering and permitting compliance.
Shore End Operations
Cable Landing Support – East Coast Canada
Managed shore-end cable landings including diver support, ROV trenching, and jointing operations in challenging tidal environments. Achieved on-time delivery with zero safety incidents.
Pipeline Installation
Deep Trenching – Baltic Sea
Subsea trenching and burial support for a new interconnector pipeline. Designed and installed custom trenching tools to manage glacial till seabed conditions and meet specified burial depths.
Asset Removal
ROV Decommissioning – Gulf of Mexico
Decommissioning of subsea structures, cutting and recovery of flowlines, and plugging of wells. Employed heavy-duty ROV tooling and debris management plans to ensure safe, environmentally compliant removal.
Shipwreck Salvage
Salvage of WWII Vessel – Pacific
High-profile WWII wreck salvage operations in the Pacific. Precision navigation, 3D sonar mapping, and custom lift bags enabled recovery of sensitive artifacts from >2,500 m depth.
AUV Survey
Autonomous Environmental Survey – Norwegian Fjords
Long-endurance AUV environmental baseline surveys in Norwegian fjords. Delivered water-column chemistry profiles, seabed mapping, and biodiversity indexing to client scientists and regulators.
Mooring & FPSO
Subsea Anchor Installation – West Africa
Engineered and executed installation of subsea anchors and mooring chains for FPSO operations offshore West Africa. Provided load testing, subsea inspection, and as-built documentation.
Latitude Operations
Hybrid ROV/AUV Survey – Arctic
Hybrid ROV/AUV systems in Arctic waters for high-latitude pipeline inspection. Managed ice cover logistics, extended tether lengths, and cold-weather adaptations for vehicles and sensors.
Telecoms
Shore-End Cable Landing – Mediterranean
Nearshore ROV support for multiple fiber optic cable landings. Plough operations, diver support, and environmental monitoring in shallow coastal zones with strict permitting windows.
Academic Partnerships
Research Collaboration with University Labs
Collaboration with universities on subsea robotics and sensing. Hosted interns on offshore campaigns, supported joint funding bids, and shared curated datasets for peer-reviewed research.
Renewable Energy
Offshore Wind Farm Cable Installation – UK
ROVs and trenching systems for inter-array and export cable installation at offshore wind projects in the North Sea. Focus on cable protection, fatigue mitigation, and burial verification to OEM specs.
FPSO Inspection
Inspection of Floating Production Units – Brazil
Routine and special-purpose inspections of FPSO subsea systems offshore Brazil. Delivered UT thickness readings, CP surveys, and high-resolution video for integrity management decisions.
Telecoms
Emergency Cable Repair – Atlantic
Rapid emergency mobilization to repair critical fiber optic cables in the Atlantic. Achieved restoration under harsh winter conditions with tight weather windows and challenging sea states.
Oil & Gas
Pipeline Maintenance – North Sea
IMR campaigns for subsea pipelines in the North Sea. Included valve operations, spoolpiece replacement, anode installation, and debris clearance to maintain system reliability.
AUV Survey
Environmental Baseline Survey – Gulf of Mexico
AUV-mounted sensors collected environmental baseline data across multiple lease blocks. Delivered 3D bathymetry, sediment chemistry, and habitat maps to support EIA submissions.
Shipwreck Archaeology
Deepwater Wreck Identification – Caribbean
ROV-assisted wreck identification and artifact assessment in the Caribbean. Produced ultra-HD imagery and multibeam mosaics for academic and government partners.
